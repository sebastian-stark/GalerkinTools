template<unsigned int dim, unsigned int spacedim>
class DependentField< dim, spacedim >

This class is used to define an interface related dependent field ( ) according to

where , , , , , , , and are constants, summation is implied over , and the notation and , respectively, indicates whether a quantity is evaluated on the + or - side of the interface (the remaining notation is introduced in IndependentField and IndependentField<0, spacedim>).

Although the class in principle also allows for higher spatial derivatives in the definition of , the documentation is restricted to first derivatives because most other parts of the GalerkinTools library make this restriction.

Template Parameters
